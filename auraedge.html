Join AuraEdge

Thousands of students across the world are looking to get the edge on not only their academics but also more advanced topics and skills. They crave more knowledge and skills, and they scour the internet for tutorials and explanations. Although the information is in abundance on the internet, locating the most effective, concise, and informative pieces are difficult; they are buried under a mountain of websites seeking profit or dedicated to other levels of education.

AuraEdge is our center for learning where students can access the most immediate and effective information they would need to achieve their goals. Students can find an assortment of information dedicated to all knowledge groups from high school to the expert level. Unlike conventional websites that offer a strong foundation in all subjects at just the high school level, we have subjects that reach into specific careers, from those in STEM to entrepreneurship. We offer introductions to python and coding, Arduino, machine learning, algorithms, neural networks, biology, hydrology, pollution, geochemistry, and applied mathematics. We also teach skills such as ethical hacking/pen-testing, business marketing, management, professionalism, college preparation, writing, literature, history, memorization, and Adobe Creative Cloud/Microsoft Office skills.

Video Graphic Content

A team of mentors, experts, and editors work hard to provide videos and podcasts for students aching to learn something new! We are also partnering up with HelmLearning (link the website) to deliver live streams from our combined mentors on their website! Videos from our mentors cover in-depth topics, and HelmLearning’s live streams offer a platform for students to communicate and ask questions with the mentors.

Challenges

Our mentors and experts also work on short challenges, such as problem sets and practice questions, for students to grasp a stronger understanding of the skills and lessons they learn. The problem sets are sent every two weeks or less on our server and website. For more advanced challenges, we offer giveaways, prizes, and sometimes a spot on our central project team! See the Interstellar Initiative for details.

The Archive

A vast archive of multiple subjects with information in the form of links, websites, servers, communities, people, and videos that our own mentors and members found useful in their own journey to learn that subject.
