COVID-19

NovaCrypt + COVID-19

Medical professionals lacked critical personal protective equipment to fight the novel coronavirus, COVID-19, and people were unable to receive the proper care and safety they needed to stay healthy. With infection rates skyrocketing and hospitals running out of supplies, immediate and swift action was needed.

Working to make a difference during these times, Project MediAir and NYFightingCovid were started to help fight the virus. NYFightingCovid is a movement based in New York’s Rockland, Orange, and Putnam Counties where we work to service the emergency services in our area. Our goal is to make, print, coordinate, and deliver crucial personal protective equipment to the medical professionals fighting at our frontlines!
