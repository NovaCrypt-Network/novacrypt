Frequently Asked Questions

A hackathon is an event that lasts several days, where students form teams to compete for prizes by building and designing projects to address specific issues, whether that be using programming, engineering, or sciences.

No worries! We’ll group you up with other competitors right before the starting of the competition, so you can get to know your teammates! A hackathon is a collaborative experience and is thus required to be in teams of at least two, and a maximum of 5.

Anyone is welcome to attend our hackathons! Whether that be virtual or physical, we would love to have you share this experience with us!

Nope, everything is paid for! From shipping prizes to food and venues, everything is paid for by our wonderful sponsors!

Just follow our Code of Conduct and Competition Guidelines here!

Nope! You can be a beginner! We have workshops you can attend, and different speakers to help you learn more skills all while experiencing the community and event!

Contact us at novacryptteam@gmail.com

    6 - 6:30 PM

    Opening Ceremony

    Some introductory notes, competition guidelines, and basic rules of conduct. We'll also introduce our speakers, workshops, booths, organizers, and judges.

    Workshops

    Enjoy workshops, lessons, and speakers from our sponsors and partners. You can also begin coding at this time, or find a team.

    Coding Session One

    It's grind time ;)

    7 AM - 8 AM

    Breakfast Break / Games

    Take a break to eat! We'll open the gaming channels during this time again.

    We'll be grading your submissions during this time. Relax, and take a well-deserved break.

    8 PM - 9 PM

    Closing Ceremony

    We'll reveal the winning teams and wrap up the competition. We'll mail out the winning prizes afterwards.
