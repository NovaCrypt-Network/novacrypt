Research + Projects


Projects

Students are more sensitive to change than ever before, being the first to notice and the first to take action. From their motivation to make their mark on society comes a determination to shape the world that they will inevitably face and lead.

The NovaCrypt Project is helmed by top student researchers, scientists, programmers, and engineers with a desire to develop new technologies and ideas that will benefit people globally. They are extremely motivated and willing to put in long hours to learn new skills and make the changes they want to see. Once individually fighting for change, their efforts now combined to tackle projects they could not face alone.

From global competitions such as ISEF and FIRST, we stood out among our competitors, as you do. Some members have already leaped directly into the scientific community and been recognized by researchers around the world for their work and journal publications. Each member’s passion for their craft paired the collaborative experience truly turned this group of unique individuals into a team.

Got what it takes? We host competitions and applications for different spots on the team! Check out the Interstellar Initiative for more details.

Got an extraordinary idea or project? We can help too! Apply to be an official project, or use our extensive network of mentors and people on our Discord server, the Interstellar Initiative, and our forums! We take no credit for your work: just credit your mentors and cite NovaCrypt in your resources!

Let’s make a difference!
