Our Partners:
Each Mind

Each Mind is a youth-run organization to change the mindset on mental health by enriching dialogue, advocating for policy reform and making treatment accessible for all. They aim to address mental health through positive media, legislative advocacy and fundraising for behavioral health clinics. Overall, their mission is to empower youth to become allies, seek help and advocate for mental health.

Mind Spark

Mind Spark is a start up organization dedicated to providing a platform where students interested in STEM can share various forms of their works to a global community. If you would like to submit your works or share your thoughts on a topic, use the link in the image to post.

United Under Arts

United Under Arts is a global student-led nonprofit organization dedicated to providing opportunities to learn about and experience the arts to all, particularly underserved populations such as children and the elderly around the world.
