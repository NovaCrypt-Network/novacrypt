About Me

I am a mentor, a speaker, an educator, and an entrepreneur. I am from India an a software eng by education. I worked in IT for 10 years and have worked in various capacities in India and outside India.
After a break of 3 yrs, I started my own company, WAVEKIDS, in the education space. We conduct sessions on hands-on-Science, drones programming, and Ardunio programming. In 6 yrs of the journey with WAVEKIDS, I have been instrumental in introducing experiential learning in various 2-tier cities in India.
I have also worked with ngos and alternate learning spaces. I have also conducted workshops across India. During COVID, I want to work in other countries as well. I have been mentoring Indian students and early stage startups.
